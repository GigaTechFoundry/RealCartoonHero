Kagome Higurashi
(日暮かごめ)

About Kagome

An anime heroine who helps a puppy destroy his brother and save the world

Characteristics

  1. Because of her empathetic, gentle heart, Kagome never hesitates to help those in need of it despite their personality, problems, race, tribe, or appearance, treating all with warmth and respect.

  2. While she is usually seen as a calm, compassionate person, Kagome also possesses a darker hidden violent side to her when her short temper is brought into play.

  3. Even when backed into a corner, Kagome figures out solutions and dares to execute it, often producing unexpected good results.
